# r/factorio community tooling, post-Space Age (2025-2026)

Factorio 2.0 + Space Age launched 2024-10-21. Eighteen months later, the
community recommendation stack has visibly re-shuffled around what was
maintained for 2.0 and what wasn't.

## In-game planning is dominated by raiguard's stack

Three of the most-recommended QoL mods for blueprint workflows --
**Rate Calculator**, **Recipe Book**, and **Bottleneck Lite** -- are all
maintained by raiguard, a Wube employee. Their 2.0 updates landed
quickly and they are the default suggestions in r/factorio "first
playthrough mods" threads. **Even Distribution** (321freddy) sits
alongside them as the fourth perennial pick; one forum thread literally
proposes bundling Rate Calculator and Even Distribution into the base
game.

## Helmod vs Factory Planner is unresolved

Helmod still has the highest download count of any planner (~521K) and
remains the choice for complex, byproduct-heavy chains (oil, Space
Exploration steam). Factory Planner (~317K, latest 2.0.50 only days
old at time of research) is recommended for newcomers because Helmod's
UI scares them off. Both are 2.0/Space Age compatible.

## Blueprint sharing: school > prints, factoriobin for quick links

For string sharing, the consensus is: **factoriobin** for "paste-and-go"
links, **factorio.school** for browseable libraries with tags and
search, **factorioprints** as a legacy archive (treated as effectively
abandoned in recent threads), and **Factorio Codex** as the rising 2.0
challenger with structure-view rendering and an integrated calculator.

## Notable casualties and gaps

- **Picker Dollies** stalled on the 2.0 update; **Even Pickier Dollies**
  is the community's chosen fork.
- **FBE (teoxoy)** is still the polished web editor of choice but lacks
  Space Age prototype rendering, which comes up repeatedly as a
  complaint.
- No widely-recommended in-browser **Space Age**-aware blueprint editor
  has emerged to replace FBE; the gap is real.
- **Discord blueprint rendering** is uniformly handled by BlueprintBot
  / Factorio-FBSR.

Spreadsheet calculators retain a small but loyal audience -- the
Lupoviridae Google Sheet has explicit 2.0 and Space Age tabs and is
still updated, though quality and new beacon math are TODO.
